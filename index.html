About Me

Cybersecurity analyst with 3+ years of experience in automotive and IT security, specializing in threat analysis, risk and vulnerability management, and incident response and digital forensics. At Continental Automotive Technologies GmbH, led Threat Analysis and Risk Assessments (TARA) in line with ISO/SAE 21434 and UN ECE R155, applying risk-based mitigation strategies to enhance embedded system resilience and ensure development-stage compliance. During my master’s thesis at Robert Bosch GmbH, designed and implemented a Digital Twin–based Intrusion Detection System (IDS) using Docker and Python to enhance threat visibility and real-time detection efficiency in industrial networks. Completed a Cybersecurity Bootcamp focused on vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, and digital forensics using Nmap, Greenbone, Metasploit, and Autopsy, developing risk-based remediation strategies to strengthen system defenses. Certified in CompTIA CySA+, Security+, and A+, with professional experience across Germany, Japan, and India. Brings a collaborative mindset and analytical approach to strengthen security operations and contribute to organizations’ missions to reduce cyber risk.
